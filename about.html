Thick Water Art is a Minnesota-based bi-monthly zine created by writers, artists, and creators of all kinds.


We focus on featuring works by unpublished or rarely-publised writers and artists. We strive to connect creators for collaborations and special projects. We encourage creativity and taking risks.
